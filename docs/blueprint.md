# **App Name**: CropDoc AI

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure user registration and login with JWT for data protection.
- Image Upload & Disease Diagnosis: Allow users to upload crop images and use an AI model tool to diagnose potential diseases, providing disease name, severity, and confidence score.
- Treatment Recommendations: Suggest treatments and preventative actions based on the disease diagnosis, integrating weather and IoT data.
- Environmental Data Integration: Fetch and display real-time weather and IoT data, such as temperature, humidity, and soil moisture, to inform disease management strategies.
- Expert Consultation: Enable farmers to connect with agricultural experts for personalized advice through a chat or video call feature.
- Dashboard: Provide a user dashboard displaying the history of uploaded images, diagnosis results, and treatment suggestions.
- Proactive Disease Management Alerts: Provide notifications based on environmental data for disease outbreaks and offer treatments based on the environmental conditions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#388E3C) to evoke nature and health.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a natural and calming feel.
- Accent color: Earthy brown (#A1887F) for highlights and important UI elements.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a little warmth and personality.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' a sans-serif with a computerized feel.
- Use clear, visually intuitive icons representing weather conditions, disease types, and treatment options.
- Clean, mobile-responsive design with a focus on accessibility and ease of use for farmers.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback and guide the user through the diagnosis process.
